## Findings

- Severity: Major
  Location: `report/sections/14_arithmetic_quantum_fields.tex:137`
  Issue: The passage chooses a symplectic basis of an arbitrary reduced field
  space and then builds the trace-character Weyl model, but the shard does not
  provide a local source or derivation for the Darboux/symplectic-basis step or
  for the exact trace-character convention used here.
  Evidence: The status anchors at `14:13-18` cite Ashikhmin--Knill
  `n9.tex:249-313` and Bombin--Martin-Delgado `HomologicalErrorCorrection6.tex:1206-1259`.
  Ashikhmin--Knill construct \(T_aR_b\) after choosing an
  \(\mathbb F_p\)-basis of \(\mathbb F_{p^m}\) and use the coordinate dot
  product \(\sum a_i b_i\) (`n9.tex:288-308`), while this shard uses
  \(\chi(t)=\exp(2\pi i\operatorname{Tr}_{F/\mathbb F_p}(t)/p)\) at `14:141`.
  `CONVENTIONS.md:266-267` records the trace-character convention, but AQM-14
  does not point to it or to a source proving that every finite symplectic
  \(F_q\)-space can be put in the displayed \(F^n\oplus F^n\) form.
  Suggested action: Add a short sourced or locally proved finite symplectic
  normal-form lemma before `L6`, and explicitly cite the trace-character
  convention/source used to replace the source's basis-dependent dot product.
  Alternatively restrict the written theorem to the coordinate model already
  fixed by convention.

- Severity: Major
  Location: `report/sections/15_projective_sheaf_fields_definitions.tex:151`
  Issue: The rationale for replacing naive projective `Hom(X,V)` by sheaf
  sections relies on a sourced claim about morphisms into affine schemes, but
  the shard's source list omits that locator.
  Evidence: Lines `15:154-156` state that a regular map to affine space is
  controlled by global regular functions. The local Stacks snapshot supports
  this at `references/algebraic_geometry/stacks_project_schemes.tex:840-963`
  (`Mor(X,Y)` for affine `Y` is bijective with maps from global functions), and
  `references/algebraic_geometry/SOURCES.md` records that locator. However the
  AQM-15 ground-truth list at `15:18-31` includes sheaves, Proj, twists,
  projective space, varieties, and \(H^0(\mathbf P^n,\mathcal O(d))\), but not
  the affine-morphism source used by the replacement argument.
  Suggested action: Add `stacks_project_schemes.tex:840-963` to the ground-truth
  list and cite it in the "Why Naive Hom Is Not the Projective Default"
  paragraph.

- Severity: Minor
  Location: `report/sections/15_projective_sheaf_fields_definitions.tex:216`
  Issue: The proof that the tensor-product pairing is alternating is incomplete
  for arbitrary sums.
  Evidence: Lines `15:224-228` check
  \(\Omega(s\otimes v,s\otimes v)=0\) on pure tensors and then say the same
  identity follows for arbitrary sums by bilinearity and alternation of
  \(\omega_V\). Pure-tensor alternation alone is not enough; the cross terms
  cancel because the scalar pairing \(B(s,t)=\sum_P w_P\operatorname{ev}_P(s)
  \operatorname{ev}_P(t)\) is symmetric and an alternating bilinear form
  satisfies \(\omega(v,w)+\omega(w,v)=0\). The proof should spell out this
  cancellation, especially since the project works over finite fields including
  characteristic \(2\).
  Suggested action: Insert the two-line expansion for
  \(\Omega(\sum_i s_i\otimes v_i,\sum_j s_j\otimes v_j)\), explicitly using
  symmetry of \(B\) and \(\omega(v,w)=-\omega(w,v)\).

## Cross-Shard Risks

AQM-14 and AQM-15 are consistent with `CONVENTIONS.md` entries `(q)` and `(r)`
on `Map(X,V)`, radical quotients, all-weights-one `F_3` examples, and
projective sheaf-section replacements. The registered run bundles and CSV
schemas for `runs/2026-05-24-arithmetic-quantum-fields/` and
`runs/2026-05-24-projective-line-sheaf-fields/` are present and match the
headline tables I checked by inspection.

The main cross-shard risk is that later shards may treat the Weyl representation
of every reduced finite symplectic \(F_q\)-space as canonical. AQM-14 currently
chooses a symplectic basis and a trace character but does not make the basis
normal-form/source dependency visible enough for downstream use.

## Suggested Actions

1. Strengthen AQM-14 `L6` with a sourced/proved finite symplectic normal-form
   statement and a precise trace-character source/convention pointer.
2. Add the Stacks affine-morphism locator to AQM-15's source list and cite it in
   the projective replacement rationale.
3. Expand the proof of AQM-15 Lemma `L2` so alternatingness for arbitrary tensor
   sums follows from explicit cross-term cancellation.
